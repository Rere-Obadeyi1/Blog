Viola! here you are. This is a quick-tour on what RERE OF LAGOS is about,

CULTURE

When it comes to culture, you can trust Lagos is Africa's melting point, as there are over 300 ethnicities here, from the Indians at Ilupeju and the Chinese communities to the Brits and Americans on the island, coming to Lagos, you can be sure of not being left out as there would be something you definitely would be able to relate to. Walking down the streeets of Yaba and indeed the island would take you down the memory lane, as the names of former colonials masters of the late 1800s to the mid-1900s and the houses they built even with the names of native Lagosians in the area and those dispersed all across the state, tell a story of this city's interaction with the British

ARTS

The artistic prowess of the Lagosian artisans stands out, from the adire pits of Bello to the beautiful art galleries at some closes, stocked with aesthetically pleasing pieces, carvings and portraits, when it comes to wheting and satisfying your appetite for things like this, Lagos is the place to be and that is why we are here to give you an artistic experience of Lagos without borders

ENTERTAINMENT

Entertainment is something you can't go wrong without and Lagos is definitely Africa's home for that, from our own genre of soothing music Afrobeats, to Apala and Fuji, the one that gets you to nod and tilt your shoulder forward and back in rhythmic fashion are regulars here, whether you are at the bus parks waiting for any of the yellow buses(danfo)

COMMUNITIES

For prospective visitors,enthusiasts and residents link up with friends right here on Rere of Lagos.
