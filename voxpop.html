3rd July 2022

LAGOS; A term synonymous with fruition in resilience.


"Ojuelegba! Ojuelegba!" the bus conductor will shout at the top of his voice, gesturing to both the waiting and the briskly walking passersby to get in, sometimes in increasing frustration after shouting for nearly 30 minutes will be seen nearly pleading with them, as though it will compel them to change plans and switch routes.


That was his day-to-day norm, a means to his breaking of bread at even,grasping old notes so tightly with sweaty palms then tucking it into his creased shirt, mindless of what commuters will think, this was the daily hustle. Like the phrase often used on the streets, "I carry my hustle for my head..."
